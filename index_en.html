Jennifer Andrášová

Jennifer Andrášová (2001) is currently a photography student at Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Bratislava. Her work contains digital and analogue photographs, portraits, mostly still lifes that capture nostalgic vibe moments and also reportage photography. Some photo projects may contain photographs that look like they have been taken in the past century and that exactly is the author’s intent.

The pictures are there, and you just take them.

Robert Capa
